About Us

Mad Ink Art Studio blends classic artistry with contemporary techniques. Our skilled artists are passionate about their craft, and we pride ourselves on creating an unforgettable experience for each client. Our shop offers a comfortable and unique atmosphere that reflects our love for gothic and antique designs.

Meet Our Artists

Tyler Munson

Tyler Munson is a talented tattoo artist known for his incredible realism tattoos. With a keen eye for detail, Tyler brings portraits, animals, and other intricate designs to life with remarkable precision. His dedication to mastering the art of realism makes him one of the most sought-after tattoo artists in the area. Whether you're looking for a custom design or a classic portrait, Tyler ensures that every piece tells a unique and personal story.

Safira Wulfsberg

Safira Wulfsberg is an innovative tattoo artist with a distinctive style that focuses on clean lines and unique, artistic designs. She has a passion for creating tattoos that are both bold and elegant, often combining geometric patterns with organic elements. Safira's attention to detail and her mastery of clean line work make her tattoos stand out. Each piece is thoughtfully crafted, creating a timeless design that reflects the individual client's personality and style.
